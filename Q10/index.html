02 About Us

Founded in 2018, Horizon Studio was built on the belief that great design is not just how something looks — it's how it works, how it feels, and the story it tells. We are a small, focused team of designers, strategists, and storytellers based in Lisbon, Portugal.

We believe in slow work and fast results. Every decision we make is deliberate. We take on a limited number of projects each year so we can give each one the full weight of our attention. Quality over volume, always.
